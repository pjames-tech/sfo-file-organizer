## How to Use

### Option 1: Desktop App (Easiest!)

- Double-click the **"File Organizer"** shortcut on your Desktop
- Or double-click `Start File Organizer.bat`

**In the app:**

1. Click **"Browse..."** to select a folder (like your Downloads folder)
2. Click **"Preview Changes"** to see what will happen
3. Click **"Organize Now"** to organize your files!
4. Made a mistake? Click **"Undo Last"** to restore everything

### Option 2: Web Dashboard

- Double-click the **"File Organizer Dashboard"** shortcut on your Desktop
- Or double-click `Open Dashboard.bat`
- Your web browser will open with the dashboard

### Option 3: Standalone App (Portable)

- If you built the executable, look in the `dist` folder
- You can copy **"Smart File Organizer.exe"** to a USB drive or any other computer
- No installation required! Just double-click to run.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it safe?

Yes! The organizer:

- Only moves files, never deletes them
- Creates category folders in the same location
- Has "Undo" to reverse any changes

### What if I make a mistake?

Click the **"Undo Last"** button to restore all files to their original locations.

### Can I preview before organizing?

Yes! Click **"Preview Changes"** to see what will happen without actually moving anything.

### Where do my files go?
